Jewellery Privilege

How your Jewellery Privilege works.

Your reserve amount is adjustable against eligible Menorah jewellery/products after 330 days.

Your jewellery privilege depends on your reserve tier and eligible final purchase value.

The benefit is not paid in cash. It is adjusted only inside the jewellery purchase.

Eligible Jewellery Privilege = Eligible Purchase Value x Plan Privilege Rate, capped at the plan maximum.
Example Calculation

Classic Reserve example.

If the customer purchases eligible jewellery worth ₹1,00,000 or more after 330 days, they may receive the full ₹6,000 Menorah Jewellery Privilege.

If the purchase value is ₹75,000, the privilege is calculated as ₹75,000 x 6% = ₹4,500. The customer receives ₹4,500 jewellery privilege, not ₹6,000.

The benefit is not paid in cash. It is adjusted only against eligible Menorah jewellery/products.

Important Terms

Menorah Future Jewellery Reserve Plan is a jewellery advance booking plan only. This is not an investment scheme, deposit scheme, interest-bearing plan, gold-return scheme, savings scheme, or cash-return plan.

The reserve amount and eligible Menorah Jewellery Privilege are redeemable only against eligible jewellery/products from Menorah Gold LLP.

No cash return, interest, profit, guaranteed gold appreciation, or fixed gold quantity is offered under this plan.

Gold rate will be applicable on the date of final purchase/redemption unless separately confirmed in writing by Menorah Gold LLP. Final GST invoice will be issued at the time of jewellery/product delivery.
