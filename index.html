About The Plan

Plan your future jewellery purchase with clarity.

The Menorah Future Jewellery Reserve Plan is a jewellery advance booking plan for customers who are planning to buy jewellery in the future.

Customers reserve an amount today with Menorah Gold LLP and become eligible to redeem it after 330 days against eligible Menorah jewellery/products.

This plan is suitable for bridal jewellery, engagement jewellery, family jewellery, gold jewellery, diamond jewellery, custom jewellery, gift jewellery, and future jewellery planning.

Important Terms

Jewellery advance booking plan only.

Menorah Future Jewellery Reserve Plan is a jewellery advance booking plan only. This is not an investment scheme, deposit scheme, interest-bearing plan, gold-return scheme, savings scheme, or cash-return plan.

The reserve amount and eligible Menorah Jewellery Privilege are redeemable only against eligible jewellery/products from Menorah Gold LLP. No cash return, interest, profit, guaranteed gold appreciation, or fixed gold quantity is offered under this plan.

Gold rate will be applicable on the date of final purchase/redemption unless separately confirmed in writing by Menorah Gold LLP. Final GST invoice will be issued at the time of jewellery/product delivery. Hallmarking, HUID, certification, and product documentation will be provided wherever applicable as per product category and legal requirements.

  • Redeemable after 330 days from the booking date.
  • Benefit adjusted only against eligible Menorah jewellery/products.
  • No cash return, interest, profit, or fixed gold quantity.
  • Final GST invoice issued at product delivery.
  • Hallmarking/HUID details provided wherever applicable.

Redemption

Redeem against eligible Menorah products.

Customer can redeem after 330 days from the booking date. The reserve amount and eligible Menorah Jewellery Privilege can be used against gold jewellery, diamond jewellery, bridal jewellery, custom jewellery, silver articles, and approved Menorah products.

The privilege may not apply to gold coins, gold bars, bullion, low-margin products, special-discount products, or products specifically excluded by Menorah Gold LLP.

Example Calculation

Classic Reserve example.

If a customer reserves ₹50,000 under Classic Reserve, the plan benefit is up to ₹6,000 Menorah Jewellery Privilege. Redemption eligibility is after 330 days.

Full Privilege Example

Purchase of ₹1,00,000 or more

If the customer purchases eligible jewellery worth ₹1,00,000 or more, they may receive the full ₹6,000 jewellery privilege.

Proportionate Example

Purchase of ₹75,000

₹75,000 × 6% = ₹4,500

The customer gets ₹4,500 jewellery privilege, not the full ₹6,000.
